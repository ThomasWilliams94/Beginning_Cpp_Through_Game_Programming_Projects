Pseudocode for the TicTacToe program: obtained from "Beginning C++ Through
Game Programming, Edition Three by Micahel Dawson"

==========================================================================

Create an empty Tic-Tac-Toe board
Display the game instrutions
Determine who goes first
Display the board
While nobody's won and it's not a tie
	If it's the human's turn
		Get the human's move
		Update the board with the human's move
	Otherwise
		Calculate the computer's move
		Update the board with the computer's move
	Display the board
	Switch turns
Congratulate the winner or declare a tie.
